Frequently Asked 04
What does a typical engagement look like?

It starts with a discovery call where we map out your goals, constraints, and timeline. From there, I put together a proposal with a clear scope, deliverables, and pricing. Most projects kick off within a week of agreement.

Do you work with teams or solo?

Both. I can embed with your existing team as a technical lead or architect, or I can own a project end-to-end as a solo practitioner. For larger initiatives, I occasionally bring in trusted collaborators from my network.

Are you available for remote work?

Yes. I work remotely with clients across time zones. I'm comfortable with async communication and flexible scheduling. For local clients, in-person workshops and onsite days are also an option.

What if I'm not sure what I need yet?

That's perfectly fine. Many of my best projects started as open-ended conversations. Reach out with whatever context you have, and we'll figure out the right approach together during the discovery call.

Do you take on pro bono or open-source work?

Selectively, yes. If you're working on something with genuine community impact or an interesting open-source project, I'm happy to discuss reduced-rate or contributed work. Just mention it in your message.
